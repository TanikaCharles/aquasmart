Water Wasted Brushing Teeth

Your citizens are always leaving the sink on while they brush their teeth. This wastes HUGE amounts of water. What will you do about it?

About

Our application is an interactive simulation game where the user is the mayor of their own city. They must make decisions about how the population can conserve water in order to make their water reserves last through the summer. We hope this game can help raise awareness of the importance of water conservation in the Vancouver area.
